Frequently Asked Questions

What types of transport services do you offer?

We provide a range of transport services including local, intercity, and international logistics. Our services cover personal travel, cargo delivery, and business transportation solutions.

How do I book a transport service?

You can book our services through our website, mobile app, or by contacting our customer service line. Bookings can be made in advance or on-demand depending on availability.

What safety measures do you have in place?

We prioritize safety by ensuring our vehicles are regularly inspected, drivers are professionally trained, and real-time GPS tracking is available for all trips.

Can I track my shipment?

Yes, all shipments come with a unique tracking ID that you can use on our website or app to monitor the real-time status and estimated delivery time.

What are your operating hours?

Our services are available 24/7 for local and intercity transport. For international services, please refer to specific operating schedules on our website or contact our support team.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods including credit/debit cards, digital wallets, bank transfers, and cash payments for certain services.

Do you offer insurance for shipments?

Yes, we offer optional insurance coverage for all shipments to ensure your cargo is protected against potential damages or loss during transit.

How can I contact customer support?

You can reach our customer support via phone, email, or live chat on our website. Our team is available 24/7 to assist you with any inquiries or issues.
